About Food 4 Adventure

Too many go out on adventures without the proper nutrition to get them through!

Let us do the planning for your next adventure. Select the hike or trip you are planning to attend. We'll make sure your group is well fed for your trip. Some of the ways we can help:

  • Calculates total calories and weight of the food plan.
  • Create a calories needed estimator based on a number of factors including weight of pack, length and difficulty of trail.
  • Opportunity to purchase food from the site.
  • Deliver that food to you or your client.
